Manifesto

Not art history. Not a cover. Not a remix.

Afterimage is an audiovisual archive where paintings are not explained, but reactivated.

Each artwork is treated as a residual signal: a human trace that survives the act of looking and returns as sound, movement, voice, and light.

The project imagines a future intelligence trying to recover the emotional frequency left by a painting in humanity's memory.

The Process

Image to frequency

Image Story Emotion Music Lyrics Motion Afterimage

For each painting, the project extracts an emotional structure, translates it into a deep electro / hybrid musical architecture, then generates visual motion synchronized with the track: stems, lyrics, stops, drops, and symbolic geography.

Definition

What is an afterimage?

An afterimage is what remains when the eye has stopped seeing. Here, it becomes a method: recovering the emotional frequency left by a painting in humanity's memory.
